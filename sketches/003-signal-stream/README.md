## Variant: Signal Stream

### Design stance
A cleaner productivity/feed hybrid. The curriculum engine pins the day’s thesis and converts posts into challenges.

### Key choices
- Layout: Twitter-like feed with learning scaffolding.
- Interaction: Expandable posts, topic filters, save/build toasts.
- Content: Platform engineering and AI engineering tutors as creators.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: making learning feel alive and scrollable.
- Weak at: not yet a real backend or personalization engine.

### Best for
- Best if Twutor should feel like a serious daily learning product.
