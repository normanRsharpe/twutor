## Variant: Tutor Feed Dark

### Design stance
Make Twutor feel less like a polished learning dashboard and more like a living expert feed: dark mode, tutor personas, social proof, mixed media, quote debates, polls, and build challenges.

### Key choices
- Layout: X/Twitter-like three-column shell with left nav, central timeline, and right rail.
- Identity: recurring tutor accounts with generated SVG avatars, handles, badges, and opinions.
- Content: mixed post shapes: hot take, diagram, quote-tweet, poll, code/trace card, build challenge.
- Interaction: ask composer, freshness bar, tab switching, follow buttons, poll feedback, save/build affordances.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: making the feed feel alive, social, and addictive while staying educational.
- Weak at: still static HTML; avatars are SVG placeholders rather than high-end generated images.

### Best for
- The likely product direction: Twitter engagement grammar adapted to AI/platform engineering tutoring.
